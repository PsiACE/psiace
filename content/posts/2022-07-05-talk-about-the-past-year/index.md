This is PsiACE. I was supposed to keep writing the iteration notes called "Decameron", but more than a month has passed since the previous issue, and I cannot really bring myself to continue.

So let this be a special issue. After all, 2022 is already halfway through, and I have also been moving along my career path for about a year. It feels like an interesting checkpoint.

## Small Milestones Worth Being Happy About

**Personal**

- Reducing carbs + taking more walks = a weight-loss plan. It has been fairly effective. I have lost about seven or eight jin, and it feels like returning to a standard build is within reach.
- I have gradually started effective schedule and time management. The key step seems to be recording rather than tracking.

**Open source**

- [@PsiACE](https://github.com/psiace/) has accumulated 233 followers, and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/repsiace) also has 300+.
- My commits on [datafuselabs/databend](https://github.com/datafuselabs/databend/) exceeded 400.
- [dataslime/ritelinked](https://github.com/dataslime/ritelinked) reached 100k downloads, and some other friends are using it now.
- [PsiACE/databend-internals](https://github.com/PsiACE/databend-internals) seems to have been noticed recently and currently has 160 stars.
- I started serving as a mentor for [@casbin](https://github.com/casbin) in GSoC, and also helped push Databend to participate in [OSPP](https://summer-ospp.ac.cn/).

## Small Troubles of a Young Person

**Life**

- Maybe I have been stuck at home for too long. I am not very active as a person, and many interesting ideas have been put aside.
- I miss my girlfriend, I miss my girlfriend, I miss my girlfriend. The pandemic is really annoying.
- My sleep quality is quite good, but I always feel like I do not sleep enough, and insomnia is also fairly serious.

**Career**

- For a few months, my state was relatively low. Although I produced databend-perf and databend-internals during that time, I am honestly not that satisfied.
- [databend-perf](https://perf.databend.rs/) has been online for a few months. It has exposed quite a few bugs and has recently gone on strike again.
- Some time ago, [@BohuTANG](https://github.com/BohuTANG/) asked about my growth during the first year after joining, and mentioned the sense of ownership. I feel that I should learn more from [@Xuanwo](https://github.com/Xuanwo) and [@flaneur2020](https://github.com/flaneur2020).

**Work**
