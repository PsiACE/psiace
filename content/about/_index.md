I'm Chojan Shang (PsiACE), an open source engineer based in Hangzhou, China.
I work at the intersection of data infrastructure and generative AI.

## Now

**Senior Engineer @ OceanBase (Ant Group).**
I lead the development of [AgentSeek](https://github.com/ob-labs/agentseek),
building database-native agent capabilities and open source tooling around the OceanBase ecosystem,
and working with the community on upstream / downstream collaboration.

## Before

**GenAI Platform Engineer @ NebulaGraph (2024 – 2025).**
Owned the engineering of the NebulaGraph GenAI platform.
Co-designed and shipped Fusion GraphRAG, reaching ~95% answer accuracy in selected scenarios
and cutting time-to-first-answer by 80% over public baselines under the same compute.

**Core Engineer @ Databend Labs (2021 – 2024).**
Founding team member of the cloud-native data warehouse Databend.
Led major refactors, modular decomposition, compile-time performance work,
and the first continuous benchmark.
Drove integrations with HuggingFace, lakeFS, and KubeSphere across data lake and cloud-native scenarios.
On the community side, grew GitHub stars from 800+ to 8000+
and served multiple years as the OSPP community liaison.

## Open source

- **ASF Member.** Committer / contributor to several Apache projects.
  Helped take **Apache OpenDAL** from incubation to graduation —
  now adopted at scale by LanceDB, Milvus, and others.
- **[ACP Python SDK](https://github.com/agentclientprotocol/python-sdk).**
  Creator and maintainer of the Agent Client Protocol Python SDK,
  with around 10M monthly downloads and adoption in products from Kimi and Mistral.
- **[bub](https://github.com/bubbuild/bub).** Core maintainer of an open source agent framework
  built for real-world multi-party collaboration.

## Speaking & writing
